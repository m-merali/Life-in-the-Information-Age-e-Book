How Communication has changed my life?

Communication has greatly affected my life. Below I will list some examples of Communication and describe how they have affected my life.

  1. Smart Phones: Smart Phones have become very powerful and, also the operating system (such as IOS, Android) that they contain can perform many meaningful tasks. I use a smart phone to mainly chat with my friends and relatives on Facebook using the messenger app on Android and make calls to my parents, friends and family. Sometimes I use the "WhatsApp" application on Android to text with my friends and relatives. I also use a smart phone to view my emails. Without a smart phone, my life after school hours would be very lonely.
  2. Email: I mostly receive emails from websites I have subscribed to. These websites include instructables.com, newscientist.com, mozilla.org etc. The emails from these websites notify me of any updates hence keeping me informed of all the events occurring on the Internet and in the world.
  3. Skype: This is the most important tool of communication in my life. I use Skype to make free calls to my relatives who live overseas i.e. in places such as New Zealand and Canada. It saves me a lot of money since the charges to make calls overseas in Kenya are very high. If it wasn't for Skype, I wouldn't be able to keep in touch with my relatives who live overseas. I also use Skype to discuss homework with my classmates. Skype is truly, a savior when it comes to communication.
