Life in the Information Age

Civil Rights

Civil rights affect everyone, they occur in a lot of the things you would do in your life.

The main focus has been on I-d cards; they have been in the news frequently and have caused controversy because the government want the cards to identify terrorists and try to prevent crime. But some people feel that the cards would be taking away some of their rights, to them the cards label people taking away their identity.

There is a lot of technology that has gone into the cards. To create an I-d card the owner needs to have a retinal scan, photo and finger prints taken. The card will have a lot of information including,

  • Photo
  • Finger prints
  • Retinal scan
  • Date of birth
  • Family information
  • And more.

To produce the cards the use of special techniques are necessary to prevent fakes to made and succeed in working as the real thing. If a fake works it makes the reason for making I-d cards a complete waste of time because people can say they are someone else and this means that someone could get away with carrying out a crime.
