Life in the Information Age

Banking and Shopping

Fifty-three million people, or 44% of internet users and one-quarter of all adults, now say they use online banking.

 A lot of technological advancements in the information age have been beneficial to all of the new generation and some of the older generation because something that minimizes the effort put in by human is always a good thing because it just makes life easier and simple for that person. For example banking online always reduces the effort of a human of having to go to the bank and waiting in a queue to be able complete his task rather he can manage most of from home except for depositing money in the bank. Shopping online benefits a human in similar ways as well so the overall effect is actually really good to the people living in the information age as it always good to advance and do something new. In Kenya a service called "MPesa" which means Mobile Money is used by everyone in the country. All you need is an agent and a number that has been registered and money can be transferred from one user o another, money can be withdrawn from your account and bills can be paid.
