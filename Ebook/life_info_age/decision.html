Life in the Information Age

Decision Making

How decision making has changed my life?

Recently, I had visited a sports store and I bumped into a pair of boots. I wanted to check whether the boot was an official product. But, unfortunately I couldn't, because the boot was completely sealed and the owner of the store wasn't helpful enough to let me open the seal. So, I googled the boot and I was linked to the official website of the company that had produced the boot. Surprisingly, the boot was for about $20 and that converted to Kenyan shillings is only 2000 but the store was selling the boot for 11000. So thanks to the Information Age I DECIDED not to buy the boot from the store as it was too expensive rather I bought it from the online store and saved allot of money.

The above is a true real life example of how ICT affects the way we make decisions.
