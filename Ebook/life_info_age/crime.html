Crime and Crime prevention

A crime is when you commit an act which is against the law. This act is punishable. Crime prevention is the effort to reduce crime and criminals. It is applied specifically to efforts made by governments to reduce crime, enforce the law, and sustain criminal justice. In this day and age, there are many ways to prevent crimes as CCTV (closed-circuit television), has been invented where there are cameras everywhere and people can watch you from a screen. In certain places around school there are CCTV cameras, mostly in the computer rooms and around the outside of the buildings. This surveillance equipment is used to watch over areas where problems are likely to take place. The CCTV cameras in the computer room are monitored in the ICT technicians’ room. Sometimes you don’t even know the cameras are there.
