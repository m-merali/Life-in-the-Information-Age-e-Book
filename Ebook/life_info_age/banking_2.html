Banking and Shopping

How online shopping has changed our lives?

Constant connectivity, contextual relevance, and a multi-screen world are changing both online and offline shopping. As the digital and in-store experiences blur, it is opening up exciting new possibilities for forward-thinking retailers. Sridhar Ramaswamy, who as Google's SVP of Ads and Commerce oversees the technology behind Google Shopping, explains how combining classic retail truths with digital savvy can help retailers do what matters most: serve their customers better.
Consumers no longer see a distinction between online and offline shopping. Whether it’s searching on a laptop, browsing main street shops or hanging out at the mall — it’s all shopping. To adapt to the competitive new reality, smart retailers are drawing on classic retailing truths of the past and augmenting them for the now. Online shopping has changed the lives of many people, for example, people live very busy lives especially those in the city and therefore they do not have time to go for shopping. So, online shopping is their only option. With online shopping, they can buy anything they want at anytime of the day, so if you arrived home late and didn't feel like cooking, you could order a pizza online and your dinner problem would be solved!. Did you see the power of online shopping?

Examples of online shopping

  • Purchasing football shoes from a website
  • Booking airline tickets online i.e. e-ticketing.
  • Buying Digital content such as movies, music, games (Angry Birds), softwares etc.
  • Buying electronics and mobile phones.
