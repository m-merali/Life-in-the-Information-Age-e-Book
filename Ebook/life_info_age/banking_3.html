Advantages

Disadvantages
Convenience- One can shop and bank money at any time Risk of your account being hacked or online fraud via phishing attacks.
Cheaper products- online there are a range of products which can be cheaper to purchase online rather than going to the store. Online banking doesn't provide quality personal service. This is contrary to local banks which provide prompt assistance when needed.
Better control over finances Actual transactions are only made during normal banking hours.
Privacy, and not having to talk to anybody Identity theft can take place.
More information available online than in branch the product does not arrive the day you order it most of the times, some companies charge for postage/shipping
There is no rush where as if u were to go shopping there would be a lot of people coming in your way  
More bank services available online  

Given the above advantages and disadvantages, I would like to say that online banking has ultimately reduced a great deal of burden. Although disadvantages exist, they could be controlled by carrying out further research on securing data online i.e. using 128 bit encryption to encrypt sensitive data such as passwords on databases. This would make it impossible for hackers to decrypt data when it is on transit.
