Digital Divide

Benefits and Drawbacks of bridging the Divide

Drawbacks

  1. People in developing countries suffer from profound starvation, malnutrition and drought problems. Logically, the governments of this countries, would focus on providing food and water to their people rather than investing in ICT. Therefore, this Countries would remain behind in the technological world and the gap would still continue to exist (perhaps, even widen). Examples of this countries include Congo (75% of the population suffering from undernourishment in 2004 - 06), Eritria 66% of the population suffering from undernourishment in 2004 - 06) etc.
