Digital Divide

What is digital Divide?

The digital divide is the gap between those people who have ready access to computers and latest technology, and those who don't have access. This can occur because of multiple factors but there are a few main factors as to why the there is a digital divide, one of them is the economical factor which affects your ability to purchase the latest technology. This can be because you may be living in a LEDC (Less Economically Developed Country) or that you are on a low salary which isn't enough to cover the costs of staying up to date with the latest technology. The less impacting factors of the digital divide are; Social, Geographical, Fear, Lack of motivation.
