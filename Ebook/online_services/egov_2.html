Online Services - Real Time Information

Advantages of Real Time Information

  • You can get hold of the latest news almost instantly on most devices providing that you have internet connection. This means that you don’t have read the newspaper and neither watch the news on television.

  • You can plan your whole trip on the internet which will give the accurate timing on your arrival and departure times and also give you the final cost.

  • You get more detailed information about things like traffic information, weather updates and sports than you would get by watching the news or reading the news.

  • You can pay for the tickets online through various payment options. This means that most likely you would be booking you money in advance allowing you to save money on the tickets by purchasing them off the internet.

Disadvantages of Real Time Information

  • Not all information is updated live which can disrupt a traveler’s journey, leading to frustration. Most of the time it is updated but sometimes it can take time for it to upload.

  • When you order the tickets of the internet and get them delivered to your house they may not always arrive before your departure date therefore you have limited proof that you booked the tickets. And if you want the tickets to some on time there is a high cost to get them delivered.

  • Online news service services provide excess information which is not always wanted by the reader whereas when you are watching the news they brief you about the main information that a reader/viewer may want to know.

  • If the news website is down for maintenance, viewers cannot view the website to find out the latest news and will have to wait until the website is back online again.
