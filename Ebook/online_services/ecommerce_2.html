Online Banking

Advantages

  • You are able to do things while at work home, or on the go, rather than physically going to banks.

  • It’s suitable for many purposes such as pay bills, write Cheques etc.

  • You can access your online bank accounts at any time during the day or night as the process is done through internet access, this service is useful as normal banking is online opened 7 days a week daily.

Disadvantages

  • It is not always secure as hacking can take place, and to repair the damages to your accounts loss, it can cost you significant amount of money.

  • Clients are more comfortable to carry out a transfer process when at a normal bank rather than online.

  • Online banking is reliant on internet. If the bank website is offline, customers are unable to access their account, and so cannot check balances. This will cause upset among many people.
