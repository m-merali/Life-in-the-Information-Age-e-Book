Disadvantages Of Online Shopping

  • Customers may feel uncomfortable when going to a transaction process and entering their credit card details, as they may think it is unsecure.

  • Customers can not feel or look at the appearance of the products they purchase online.

  • Products may take long to be delivered, or may be damaged through transport.
