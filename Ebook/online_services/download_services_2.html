Online Services - Download Services

Music

Advantages of Download Services

  • It is mostly time efficient as songs don't need to be downloaded every time. Also no internet connection is needed to stream the song once it has been downloaded

  • It is cost effective as you only need to pay $5 every month and get access to all songs rather than buying an album for around $40

Disadvantages Of Download Services

  • Music files are illegally downloaded by people via torrents and other illegal websites. Due to this, music industries have suffered major losses.

  • The quality of a music file downloaded from the Internet is not the same as its original CD.

In conclusion, downloading music online is far more convenient than visiting a music store physically. The only major drawback is piracy which can be tackled by developing new methods of music distribution e.g. by creating a music file format that can only be played on the computer of the person who has purchased the music. In this way, music piracy will reduce drastically because people will be left with no option, but, to purchase the music file.
