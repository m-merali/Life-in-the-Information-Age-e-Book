Online Gaming

Advantages Disadvantages
The main advantage of online gaming is that it offers multiplayer compatibility. Playing online with tough opponents is more exciting and interesting compared to playing with the computer. You also get to win a title of being the best in a certain online game. Online gaming due to its addictive nature, acts as a hindrance to school or office work and also in some cases affects your social life. It draws you away from mandatory activities that are beneficial to your life such as sports.
There are myriad of online games to choose from, so you can never get bored of playing online games The fact that you play with people from all around the world, they might be a possibility of you being mocked or being bullied by other people.
It improves your gaming skills since you play with other players who are professionals in gaming. Some games don't give the age bracket hence children can be accidentally exposed to games with sexual content, violence and profane language

None-Internet alternative

The far best non-Internet alternative to online gaming, especially in my local area is to go to a "Game zone". It is a place where people of all ages gather at a place (mostly a small shop) and play multiplayer games with each other on consoles such as Xbox, PS3 and PS2. It is more exciting since the environment is vibrant and also you get to be more social i.e. make new friends.

Thanks to the Internet, we are now able to play games with different people around the world. The only problem that arises while playing games online is trying not to get glued to the screen (I know it's addictive). However, you can go about this by playing sports or carrying out other activities that deter you from playing online games.
