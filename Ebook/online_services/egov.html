Online Services - Real Time Information

Real-time information is information that is updated live as time passes so that people can find out the latest information whenever they want to. Such services as this is mainly known as the news but can also be known as weather update which is technically part of the news and other things like train time tables and also traffic reports. This information is based on peoples everyday activities as these can disrupt people’s day to day activities, so real time information basically allows you to plan ahead. Most of the time this information is used such as from the news to find out what is going on nearby and also international information.

NEWS SERVICES:

This news service often comes in three formats where you can stream over the internet the latest news or you can read the news directly from a website or download the app for it on a digital device if supported.  For Example: BBC News, Sky News etc

TRAIN TIMETABLES

This is a service where you can get live information update from the internet on train departures and arrivals as well as costs for the train tickets. You can also get information about any maintenance work that may be going on and also even plan your route. www.thetrainline.com
