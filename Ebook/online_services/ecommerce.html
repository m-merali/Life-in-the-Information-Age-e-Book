Online Banking

Online banking is when clients use internet to browse their own accounts information, an online banking system offer customers just about every service, you can transfer between accounts and pay bills online. Also for the past recent years online banking was introduced it has become common that many customers tend to go online and browse their account easily.

Online banking provides 24/7 secure access to your bank accounts. Online banking enables you to carry out the following:-

  •  View your bank account balances and statements online.
  • Apply for Loans, bank drafts, credit cards and telegraphic transfers.
  • Transfer funds between accounts.
  • Settle your bank account balances, credit card bills and insurance premiums.
  • Create, change and stop standing orders
